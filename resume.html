Mask Mayhem

In this game, each player takes turns launching themselves towards the face of the human in the center of the room. They must avoid traps and launch above and between room objects to reach the face before the other player. This game was coursework done in CS 426, in the Department of CS at the University of Illinois Chicago. I pitched the original game idea and acted as the project manager for a 3-person team.

Pineapple Cleaning Products

In this VR project in the CAVE2™ Virtual Environment in the Electronic Visualization Lab at University of Illinois Chicago, each industrial detergent interaction worsens the surrounding water. Conversely, the pineapple cleaner interactions demonstrate the steps for creating detergent with pineapple scraps, with a cleaner, livelier body of water as a result. The simplistic, familiar design of these interactions illustrates that, while it is easy to accidentally exacerbate water degradation, it is also easy to help secure sanitation and clean water. This was a interdisciplinary semester-long project with 2 graphic design students.
